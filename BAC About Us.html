WHO WE ARE

BACSEC(BAC Science Education Consultants) is a group of dedicated teachers that feel touched about the state of the education system of our country Uganda. We teach online subjects for both A and O'Level most especially Sciences which include Mathematicts, Biology, Physics, Chemistry and Agriculture.

This is a way and solution of keeping our students and children engaged in this COVID-19 lockdown. As a way of extending our services, links to various lessons are always shared before time to enable students to prepare early enough.

We also engage students in practicals of the things learnt in class through demonstrantions and assignments in fields that are in their da-to-day life and as a result, we have seen a drastic improvement in the performance and understanding of the students that have been entrusted with us.

Students are free to interact with teachers online inorder to ask anything that they mightnot get very well in class. In addithion, all our lessons are recorded and shared on You Tube where students are free to visit and replay at their convinient time and as many times as they want so asa to understand better.

We also offer guidance and counselling and career guidance to students.
